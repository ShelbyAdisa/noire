Eau de Parfum OCEAN DI GIOIA

Ocean di Gioia, the feminine fragrance by Giorgio Armani, is an homage to the free, invigorating, and multi-faceted blue expanse.

This fragrance begins with a fresh splash of energy, featuring a juicy and crispy duo of pear and citrus. At its heart, a crystalline and sophisticated floral bouquet of water jasmine, lily of the valley, and rose creates modern, invigorating salty-aquatic sensations. The fragrance finishes with a vibrant and wrapping trail, with addictive woody notes of sandalwood and musks, adding structure for a comfortable, lasting impression.

Encased in a bottle with curved lines inspired by nature, the pure, organic design is a modern embodiment of Giorgio Armani's vision of essential elegance. This fragrance was created for the lively, natural, and dazzling woman who is in harmony with herself.

Dive into Ocean di Gioia—a joyful, sparkling floral bouquet with modern salty-aquatic sensations, perfect for the woman who radiates confidence and grace.

"A fragrance that captures the soul of the ocean, the spirit of the modern woman." – Noire Luxe x Armani
