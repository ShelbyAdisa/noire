Eau de Parfum TERRA DI GIOIA

Terra di Gioia, the new feminine fragrance by Giorgio Armani, is a tribute to the warm, sensual, and enveloping Mediterranean basin.

This evocative fragrance captures the enchanting mood of the Mediterranean, where voluptuous dunes meet amber rocks bordering vast seas.

The scent opens with a sparkling and tender pear accord, fused with fresh citrus notes. At its heart lies a fruity and velvety jasmine grandiflorum from India, crafted exclusively for Armani. The fragrance’s base is enriched with musks and ambery woods, creating a soft and enveloping sillage.

Housed in a bottle with curved lines inspired by nature, the organic design is a modern embodiment of Giorgio Armani’s vision of essential elegance. It represents a timeless search for authentic femininity, reflecting the very essence of Armani’s inspiration.

"A radiant tribute to Mediterranean warmth and elegance." - Noire Luxe x Armani
