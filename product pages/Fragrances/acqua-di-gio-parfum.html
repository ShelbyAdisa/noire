Acqua di Giò Parfum

Acqua Di Giò Parfum brings an intense freshness to the collection. Its top notes blend bergamot heart, ginger, and the intensity of marine notes. At its heart, geranium bourbon, rosemary essence, and clary sage heart fuse to create a vibrant aromatic signature. A base of vibrant woods, patchouli essence, and incense adds depth and mystery.

This powerful fragrance is distinguished by its incense notes, which bring an incandescent intensity to the emblematic marine accords of Acqua di Giò. A new preciousness is reflected in the bottle’s design—its sleek, masculine form, crafted in graduated black tones.

As part of Giorgio Armani's commitment to sustainability, this fragrance is refillable in all formats with a 150ml refill bottle. Its eco-conscious design extends beyond the refillable bottle, incorporating natural and sustainably sourced ingredients, making it a refined and responsible choice.

"A bold statement of refined masculinity, balancing power and elegance." - Noire Luxe x Armani
