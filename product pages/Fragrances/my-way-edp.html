Eau de Parfum MY WAY 90 ml

My Way is an invitation to open your horizons and experience meaningful encounters all over the world. A luminous bouquet of white flowers is enclosed in a bottle that resembles a talisman: a metaphor for the world. MY WAY contains discoveries and connections dedicated to a curious, authentic woman in search of experiences that challenge preconceptions.

This feminine fragrance is made from the best natural ingredients. In the top notes, Bergamot meets Orange Blossoms from Egypt, creating a brilliant luminosity. The heart opens up in a radiant bouquet of Tuberose and Jasmine from India. Finally, the base notes: a harmony composed of a sensual Premium Bourbon Vanilla from Madagascar, vibrant nuances of Virginia Cedarwood and delicate White Musks.

"The blue and gold cap is a metaphor for the world. Blue is the universal color, which unites each of us. It is surrounded by a gold ring, engraved with the signature 'Giorgio Armani', to symbolize a journey enriched by every single encounter."

This luxurious fragrance is refillable. Remember a talisman that encompasses all meaningful encounters. I AM WHAT I LIVE.
