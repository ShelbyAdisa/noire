Eau de Parfum MY WAY INTENSE 90 ml

A floral bouquet, powerful and luminous, with a woody and penetrating trail that contains unique experiences and authentic encounters lived around the world. Live your way, intensely. My Way Intense invites you to set out to discover yourself, on a journey made up of encounters full of meaning and important experiences.

An amplified florality on a persistent woody trail, based on ingredients from sustainable sources. The fragrance opens on bright notes of orange blossom from Egypt. A new, intense heart of Indian tuberose reveals a soft and velvety side of the floral signature. At the base, New Caledonian sandalwood takes on an irresistibly creamy character thanks to the encounter with the voluptuous Madagascar vanilla.

A Fragrance for the Future: MY WAY favors the use of natural ingredients, harvested in a responsible and sustainable way. Madagascar vanilla comes from local programs that support disadvantaged communities according to the principles of fair trade.

A Refillable Bottle, Designed to Last: My Way offers an innovative refill system that allows you to unscrew and refill any size at home—30, 50, or 90 ml— reducing carbon emissions by 60% and the use of plastic by 64%. A commitment to reducing the environmental footprint.

Giorgio Armani's Sustainability Efforts: Giorgio Armani engages in forest protection programs in Madagascar, Zimbabwe, Peru, and Brazil. Since its launch, MY WAY has helped protect over 14,000 hectares of primary forest to reduce its environmental footprint and preserve biodiversity. Thanks to eco-design and biodiversity protection efforts, MY WAY INTENSE achieves carbon neutrality.
