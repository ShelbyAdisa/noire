Eau de Parfum SÌ PASSIONE 50 ml

A powerful reinterpretation of the classic feminine fragrance, Sì Passione is enriched with a new dimension—an irresistible combination of grace, strength, and a spirit of independence.

An intense and decisive perfume, designed for a determined woman who dares to say yes. Encased in a vibrant red bottle, Sì Passione is a luminous and sensual fragrance that instills confidence, charm, and strength.

The fragrance opens with spicy notes of pink pepper for an invigorating adrenaline effect, while lively and radiant pear accents add a bold touch of style. The floral heart beats passionately with jasmine and rose, creating a fragrance that reflects a seemingly fragile woman capable of extraordinary strength when guided by passion.

The addition of heliotrope makes the fragrance even more irresistible, transforming it into an invitation to indulge in small luxuries and pleasures. The warmth and sensuality of vanilla and cedarwood essence bring a sense of security to the wearer—like the confidence of red lipstick.

"An intense and decisive perfume, designed for a determined woman who dares to say yes." – Giorgio Armani
